Hello, World! I am Sujin Lee from Seoul, Korea.
I write code. I play music.

After completing my undergraduate studies in Music Composition & Education, I have pursued my passions of music and programming with equal vigor, aiming to make creative work that inspires. I want to use programming to unlock the secret of music masterpiece in the nearest future. I had learned classical music since I was young, but I have been born again as python and web developer.

I am currently working in CONNECT foundation, which was established by Naver Corp. to provide software engineering education to young Koreans.

While I enjoy all aspects of my job, I run Django Girl Seoul Community with friends to encourage women to jump into technology lately and we manage workshops about programming in Python and Django.

If you want to say hello, I'm mostly on twitter or facebook. I always love hearing about you and new projects.
